PRIVACY FIRST

Privacy Policy

Your files are your business. We keep it that way.

Last Updated: July 2, 2026

1. Zero File Uploads

QIFY is built on a fundamental principle of privacy: your files never leave your device. All PDF editing, photo manipulation, and file conversions are processed entirely locally within your web browser. We do not have servers that process your files, which means it is physically impossible for us to view, store, or share your documents.

2. No Accounts or Personal Data

We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information (such as your name or email address) to use our tools. You remain completely anonymous while using QIFY.

3. Local Storage

To improve your experience, QIFY may use your browser's local storage (similar to cookies, but kept entirely on your device). We use this strictly to remember your workspace preferences, such as:

  • Your preferred export format (e.g., PNG vs JPG)
  • Quality settings
  • UI theme preferences (Dark/Light mode)

This data is never transmitted to us or any third parties. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache.

4. Analytics

We may use minimal, privacy-friendly, anonymized analytics to understand general website traffic (e.g., how many people visit the site). This does not track individual user behavior or link to any identifiable data.

5. Changes to this Policy

If we ever change how we handle privacy (though our local-first philosophy is permanent), we will update this page. Continued use of QIFY means you accept the updated policy.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, you can reach out via our designated contact channels or on our GitHub repository.
