Quick Answers

Save time by checking our most frequently asked questions before sending an email.

Do you store my files?

Never. All processing happens entirely within your browser. We don't have servers that save, view, or store your documents. Your privacy is 100% guaranteed.

Are the tools really free?

Yes! Our core document and image manipulation tools are completely free to use without any hidden subscriptions or paywalls.

Is there a file size limit?

Since the processing happens on your local device, the only limit is your computer's memory (RAM). Most modern devices can handle files up to a few hundred megabytes easily.

Do I need an account?

Nope. You don't need to sign up, log in, or give us your email to use any of the tools on QIFY. Just click and go.
